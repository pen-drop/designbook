# PetMatch

## Description
A modern pet adoption platform connecting animal shelters with potential adopters through smart matching, rich pet profiles, and post-adoption community support.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Shelters struggle to showcase available pets
Shelter websites are often outdated and hard to navigate. PetMatch provides rich pet profiles with multiple photos, personality traits, and compatibility scores that make each animal stand out.

### Problem 2: Adopters can't filter by lifestyle compatibility
Most adoption searches rely on breed or age only. PetMatch uses a smart matching algorithm that considers living situation, activity level, family composition, and experience to suggest compatible pets.

### Problem 3: Post-adoption support is lacking
Adopters are often left without guidance after bringing a pet home. PetMatch includes a built-in community forum, vet appointment scheduling, and training resources to support new pet families.

## Key Features
- Pet profile browser with advanced search and filtering
- Shelter dashboard for managing listings and applications
- Adoption application workflow with status tracking
- Smart matching algorithm based on lifestyle compatibility
- Community forum and resources for pet owners
- Vet integration and health tracking
- Responsive design optimized for mobile browsing
