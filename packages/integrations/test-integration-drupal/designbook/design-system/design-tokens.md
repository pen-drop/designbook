# PetMatch Design System

## Color Palette

A warm, inviting palette that builds trust and reflects the joy of pet companionship.

| Token | Hex | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Primary** | `#E86C3A` | Warm coral-orange — main brand, navigation, buttons |
| **Secondary** | `#2AB5A0` | Teal green — trust, nature, shelter badges |
| **Accent** | `#F5A623` | Warm amber — highlights, CTAs, star ratings |
| **Neutral** | `#1A1A2E` | Deep navy — footer, dark sections |
| **Base-100** | `#FDFBF7` | Warm cream — page background |
| **Base-200** | `#F5F0E8` | Soft beige — card surfaces |
| **Base-300** | `#E8E0D4` | Muted sand — borders, dividers |
| **Base-content** | `#2D2A26` | Warm dark brown — body text |

## Typography

| Role | Font | Character |
|---|---|---|
| **Heading** | Outfit | Rounded geometric — friendly yet modern |
| **Body** | Inter | Clean, highly readable UI standard |
| **Mono** | JetBrains Mono | Reference codes, shelter IDs |

## Design Philosophy

- **Warm tones** over cold blues — pets are about emotion and connection
- **Generous rounding** (1rem cards, pill badges) — soft, approachable feel
- **Cream base** instead of pure white — cozy, like being at home with your pet
- **Teal secondary** for trust signals — shelter verification, health status
- **Amber accent** for energy — adoption CTAs, urgency indicators
