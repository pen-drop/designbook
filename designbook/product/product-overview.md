# Keytec Website

## Description
A modern corporate website for a Drupal agency, built with Drupal and Storybook. The site presents the agency's services, team, portfolio, and expertise — designed and developed using Designbook workflows for structured, AI-guided design decisions.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Outdated Online Presence
The current website doesn't reflect the agency's technical expertise and modern approach to web development. A redesigned corporate site with a clean, contemporary design establishes credibility and attracts new clients.

### Problem 2: Unclear Service Communication
Potential clients struggle to understand what the agency offers and how it differs from competitors. Clearly structured service pages with concrete examples and case studies make the agency's value proposition immediately visible.

### Problem 3: Missing Portfolio Showcase
Completed projects and technical capabilities aren't presented effectively. A dedicated portfolio section with project details, technologies used, and results demonstrates proven expertise.

### Problem 4: Difficult Content Maintenance
Content updates require developer involvement, slowing down marketing and communication efforts. A Drupal-based CMS with well-structured content types enables the team to maintain content independently.

## Key Features
- Agency services overview with detailed service pages
- Team presentation with roles and expertise
- Portfolio showcase with project case studies
- Blog or insights section for thought leadership
- Contact and inquiry forms
- Responsive design with Drupal and Storybook component library
- SEO-optimized structure and content
